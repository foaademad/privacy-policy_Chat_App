Welcome to LanChat App

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your data and protect your rights.

1

1. Introduction

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use LanChat App. By using our service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Phone Number: We use your phone number for account registration and identity verification.

Name: We request your name to display it in chats and your profile.

Contacts: We access your contacts to sync them with the app and display contact names instead of phone numbers.

Photos & Media: We use your camera and photo library to send and receive photos and media in chats.

Microphone: We use your microphone to send voice messages and make calls.

Chat Data: We store messages and media sent and received to provide chat services.

3

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

To provide chat and calling services.

To improve user experience.

To communicate with you about the app.

To ensure security and prevent fraud.

To comply with legal obligations.

4

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following cases:

With other users in the app (such as your name and phone number to users in your contacts).

When legally required or to protect against harm.

With service providers who help us operate the app (ensuring they comply with privacy standards).

5

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

Access your personal information.

Correct inaccurate information.

Delete your account and information.

Object to processing of your information.

Request data portability.

7

7. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use technologies like cookies and local storage to provide and improve our services. You can control these settings through your device settings.

8

8. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete that information immediately.
